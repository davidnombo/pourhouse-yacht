What this comparison shows

PourHouse appears to sit at the smallest practical end of the Bluewater houseboat/coastal-cruiser market. The closest direct comparisons found in public search are other 42-foot Bluewater Coastal Cruiser listings, then the market quickly steps into 47, 51, 52, 55, 58, 59, 65, and 68-foot boats.

This page is buyer-facing but transparent: listing prices and availability can change without notice, and some public/social sources only expose snippets without full detail.

Why the 42-foot comps matter

The closest buyer comparison is not a 55- or 68-foot yacht; it is another 42-foot Bluewater Coastal Cruiser. That is why the 42-foot listings are highlighted and kept at the top.

Why PourHouse belongs near the top of this list

At 42 feet and $35,000, PourHouse is positioned as one of the shortest, most approachable Bluewater Coastal Cruiser options: wide walkaround decks, high railings, working air, two working heads/showers, full galley, twin Crusader closed-cooled gas engines, Kohler generator, and regular current use on the Ohio River.
