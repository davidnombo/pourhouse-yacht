A Bluewater built around comfort, access, and safe movement.

PourHouse’s standout feature is not a gadget — it is the layout. The wide side decks, high railings, flat social topside, and boxy interior make this boat easy to enjoy with guests of all ages.

▣

Wide walkaround decks with high railings

The boat has roughly 14-inch-plus walkaround side decks and high railings all the way around. That makes boarding, moving forward, handling lines, and hosting guests feel much more secure than on many cruisers.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Excellent family boat

Guests of many ages and body types have moved around PourHouse successfully, including wheelchair users. It is a confidence-inspiring boat for children, older guests, and people not used to being rocked around on the water.

☀

Social upper helm

The topside surface is flat and open, keeping the captain right with guests instead of separated from the conversation while driving outside or relaxing at the dock.

⌂

Simple floating-home interior

Inside, the boat feels like a large, boxy houseboat: straightforward to maintain, easy to decorate, and comfortable without a maze of steps or cramped corners.

✓

Used and maintained

PourHouse has been seasonal liveaboard-style family boating, sees regular use, and is used every weekend. All major systems are reported working, and the boat drives and steers correctly.
